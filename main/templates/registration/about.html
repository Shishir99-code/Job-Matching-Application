Accessible, Anonymous Networking

As recent Georgia Tech graduates who went through the job serach, we realized that there is a lack of transparency during the interview process about some job-related questions candidate have. To make this process easier, we offfer an an anonymous networking service to connect you to previous employees and interns to get your questions answered! Just drop in your resume and desired position, and you’ll be matched with employees of similar background to freely ask any questions you may have.
